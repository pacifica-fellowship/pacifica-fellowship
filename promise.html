Most international students come here alone

They must struggle through the adaptation process — navigating a new culture, a new language, and a new academic system entirely on their own.

With Pacifica, that is never the case. From our highly vetted counselors, top-grade tutors, and structured schedules to our daily monitoring of students, weekly check-ins, and bi-weekly updates for parents, you can rest assured that your child is being looked after to ensure the highest likelihood of success.

How we deliver on that promise

Highly Vetted Counselors

Every counselor in our program is carefully selected and background-checked, ensuring your child is guided by trusted, experienced professionals.

Top-Grade Tutors

Academic and English language tutoring tailored to each student's needs, keeping them on track for admission to a top university.

Structured Schedules

Every day is accounted for — from classes and study sessions to cultural activities — so your child stays focused and engaged.

Daily Monitoring

We check in on every student daily, ensuring they are healthy, attending classes, and progressing toward their goals.

Weekly Student Check-Ins

Dedicated one-on-one sessions each week to address challenges, celebrate wins, and keep your child on the path to success.

Bi-Weekly Parent Updates

Detailed reports every two weeks so you always know how your child is doing — academically, socially, and personally.
